## 📖 About

Green Technologies TT is an infographic-style website covering:

- What e-waste is and why it matters in Trinidad & Tobago
- The 6 categories of e-waste and their lifecycles
- Cutting-edge recycling technologies (AMR, Bioleaching, Plasma Arc, and more)
- Local recycling initiatives including the Bmobile "Help Electronics Live Longer" campaign
- An interactive quiz to test your e-waste knowledge

Built as part of MSc research: **Cutting-Edge Technologies in Electronic Recycling**
Anglia Ruskin University, 2024 · Researcher: Keturah Phillips
